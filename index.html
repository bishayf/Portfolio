Hi, I am Freddy 👋. I am a medical student with a background in psychology and mental health. I hope to become a physician who understands illness in full, not just the biology but the person behind it. That is what drew me to medicine and what keeps me in it.

I am Egyptian-Canadian, Coptic Orthodox, and Arabic-speaking. I have spent my whole life between two worlds and I am still figuring out what that means. I know it shapes how I listen. Away from medicine, I have recently fell in love with film photography, am a massive Liverpool and Raptors fan, and am a two time fantasy sports champ 🥇.
